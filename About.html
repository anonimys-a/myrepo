Jixian Wang

Jixian Wang is the CEO and Co-founder of Openbiz Technology. The Shangdi – Jade Emperor of our small developer community goes beyond an elementary implementation engagement, using the experience gained through the successful delivery execution of large-scale projects, as well as, smaller, open source projects, to recommend and implement the solutions that position you to achieve your business goals.

In 2015 he co-founded Openbiz Technology, a company located in Macedonia in cooperation with the Investor of Shenzhen XiXi Network Technology that specializes in implementation of network security on artificial intelligence.

Jixian majored in art and design at the Teacher’s College of Beijing Union University. His astonishing insights in the global IT market and software development along with his flawless sense for aesthetics had made him a true visionary.

Acting as a chief technology officer at Hong Kong’s Openbiz LLC for more than five years and Norway’s Pickatale for two and a half years helped him broaden his field of expertise, which further led to the idea of establishing a new business in Macedonia. He saw a lack of experts in the cyber security area and decided to launch such project in Europe.

Prior to the post, Jixian established the Inland Future Technology, an ISP web-hosting company, one of the top 10 providers in China with over 1mn users.
